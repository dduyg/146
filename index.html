One rainy evening, as Ji-Yoon was closing up shop, a mysterious stranger entered the bookstore. He was tall and impeccably dressed, with piercing eyes that seemed to hold secrets untold. His name was Min-Joon, and he was searching for a rare ancient tome rumored to hold the key to unlocking a long-lost legacy.

As fate would have it, Ji-Yoon possessed the very book he sought. Passed down through generations in her family, the ancient tome held a powerful enchantment that only she could unlock. Intrigued by her connection to the book, Min-Joon persuaded Ji-Yoon to accompany him on a journey to uncover the truth behind its mysterious origins.
